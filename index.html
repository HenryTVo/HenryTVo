About Me:

My name is Henry Vo and I was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona but my family is originally from Vietnam. I am currently a senior at Arizona State University majoring in Computer Information Systems and Supply Chain Management. To be honest, I don't really have a specific dream job, but I know that my ideal job would be to work in the tech industry and be able to communicate with my team on a daily basis, as I enjoy working with others as well as working on tasks on my own. Ideally, this would be at a company where I enjoy working with the people around me and the environment allows me to grow. Whether this be in software development, IT auditing, Cybersecurity, and anything else that would allow me to utilize my technical skills and knowledge. As for skills required for these positions, this would include a good understanding of programming languages (Python, HTML, JavaScript, C++, and others), a good understanding of cybersecurity, deep knowledge of network architecture, process and protocols, and operating systems for IT auditing.
